Narayanhiti Palace Museum

The place is full of artifacts from former Narayanhiti Palace and an important place where the Nepal's Royal family was massacared by yet unknown in June 1, 2001. Some of the important things to notice are Royal Cannon, the King's and Queen's bedroom full of ancient artifacts, military barrack

Road to Narayanhiti
